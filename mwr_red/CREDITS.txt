The MWR_Red theme is loosely derived from the 7dana-Clean theme. You can download the 7dana-Clean theme at the following link:

http://www.7dana.com/download/pafiledb.php?action=file&id=21

This notice was not required by 7dana, but is a courtesy and a show of respect.

If you would like to have a custom theme designed for your site, I strongly recommend visiting 7dana.com!
